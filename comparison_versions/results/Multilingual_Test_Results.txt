Query:
"What should I do after a traffic accident?"

Answer:
In the event of a traffic accident resulting in damage, you must document the scene and report it immediately.

Steps:

Contact Najm and download the Najm application.
Take photographs of the accident from all angles.
Park the vehicle away from crowds, if possible.
Determine the location of the accident, enter the data, and confirm your phone number.
Submit a report containing the circumstances, location, type of accident, and types of vehicles involved.

Key Points:

You may only move the vehicle if it obstructs traffic, poses a threat to public safety, or if the accident was simple and both parties agree on what happened.
Leaving the scene without reason is considered fleeing from justice and will result in legal action.
Failure to comply with these laws can result in a fine of up to 2,000 Saudi riyals and/or imprisonment for up to 3 months.

Q2
--
Query:
"What should a driver do when approaching a roundabout?"

Answer:
When approaching a roundabout, a driver must plan their lane in advance and give way to vehicles already traveling inside the roundabout.

Steps:

Give way to vehicles traveling around the roundabout
Plan your lane in advance based on your intended exit
Use track 1 if turning right for the first exit
Use track 2, if available, if driving straight for the second exit
Use track 3, if available, if turning left for the third exit or making a full rotation for the fourth exit
Give a signal at the roundabout

Answer:
Regarding roundabouts, priority is given to vehicles already traveling inside the roundabout.

Key Points:

A driver approaching a roundabout must give way to vehicles traveling around it.
Vehicles inside the roundabout have priority over vehicles approaching from the outside.
This priority rule applies in the absence of traffic lights or a traffic officer directing traffic.

Q4
--
Query:
"Can I let my friend drive my car without a license?"

Answer:
No, it is not allowed.

Key Points:

The rule prohibits allowing any person who does not hold a driving license to drive your vehicle.
This applies to the vehicle's owner, designated driver, or possessor.
The penalty is a fine of not less than 1,000 riyals and not more than 2,000 riyals.
If a traffic accident occurs as a result, both the owner and the unlicensed driver will be jointly liable based on the court's discretion.

References:
Traffic Law.pdf, p.20, Article 77
Article 77

Query:
"Is modifying a car allowed?"

Answer:
Multilingual
Modifying a car is not allowed without prior permission.

Key Points:

A vehicle cannot be altered by changing its color, features, standard equipment, capacity, dimensions, weight, or engine power.
This rule applies to vehicle owners, repair shop owners, and workers.
Prior valid permission must be obtained from the competent authority before making any modifications or repairing the external body.

Penalties for Repair Shops:

Repair shop owners or workers who violate this rule face a fine between 10,000 and 50,000 riyals and shop closure for up to three months.
A second violation results in a doubled fine and closure for up to six months.
A third violation results in a doubled fine and permanent closure of the repair shop.

Traffic Law.pdf, p.16, Article 64
Article 64

Query:
"وش العقوبة؟"

Answer:
تختلف العقوبة في نظام المرور بناءً على نوع المخالفة أو الحادث المرتكب.

النقاط الرئيسية:

العقوبات المتعلقة بالحوادث المرورية (التعمد أو التفريط):

في حال التسبب بوفاة أو زوال عضو أو تعطيل منفعته: السجن لمدة لا تتجاوز 4 سنوات، وغرامة لا تتجاوز 200,000 ريال، أو بإحدى هاتين العقوبتين.
في حال التسبب بإصابة تزيد مدة الشفاء منها عن 15 يوماً: السجن لمدة لا تتجاوز سنتين، وغرامة لا تتجاوز 100,000 ريال، أو بإحدى هاتين العقوبتين.
في الحالات الأخرى: تتم الإحالة للمحكمة المختصة لتحديد العقوبة المناسبة.

العقوبات المتعلقة بجداول المخالفات العامة:

تتراوح الغرامات المالية بين 100 ريال كحد أدنى وتصل إلى 6,000 ريال كحد أقصى، وذلك بناءً على فئة جدول المخالفات الملحق بالنظام.
قد تتضمن العقوبة حجز المركبة في بعض المخالفات حتى إزالة المخالفة أو في حال تعذر الوصول للسائق.

العقوبات المتعلقة بالرخص الدولية ودفاتر العبور الجمركي:

غرامة مالية لا تتجاوز 100,000 ريال لمخالفة القواعد المنظمة لها.
في حال تكرار المخالفة للمرة الأولى خلال سنة، لا تقل الغرامة عن نصف الغرامة المقررة.
في حال تكرار المخالفة للمرة الثانية خلال سنة، تضاعف الغرامة أو يتم إلغاء الرخصة، أو تطبق كلتا العقوبتين.
